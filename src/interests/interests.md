A random mix of things I spend far too much time thinking about, building, collecting, watching, listening to or tinkering with.{.lead}

## Web design and the open web

I’ve been building websites since college and still enjoy the process just as much now as I did then. I’m particularly interested in lightweight, content-first websites, design systems, accessibility and the IndieWeb movement.

I prefer simple websites that load quickly, work well and feel personal over heavily polished platforms filled with tracking and unnecessary complexity.

Things I enjoy:

* Eleventy
* Design systems
* Frontend development
* Accessibility
* Personal websites
* Static sites
* UI design
* Web performance
