## Changelog: New Collections & Logs Sections

I've added two new sections to the site: **Collections** and **Logs**.

**Collections** is home to things I collect over time rather than create. Music, Lego, Funko Pops and anything else that feels worth keeping together in one place. It's essentially a growing archive of my interests.

**Logs** is a little different. Instead of curated collections, it's a timeline of things I've been doing — what I've watched, played, read, listened to, drunk and more. Most of it updates automatically from the services I already use, so the site gradually builds a record of what I've been up to without any extra effort.

I've wanted to make the site feel more like a personal archive for a while, and these two sections are a big step towards that. There's still plenty I'd like to add, but they're in a good place to start sharing.

Take a look at the **[Collections](/collections/)** and **[Logs](/logs/)** sections.
